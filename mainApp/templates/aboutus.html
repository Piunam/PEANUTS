ABOUT US

Problem statement

Traditional coding platforms fail to provide an engaging and interactive learning experience, resulting in disengagement, slow skill development, and limited opportunities for connection and career growth among developers. How can a platform be designed to offer a comprehensive, community-driven, and competitive environment that fosters immersive learning, skill mastery, and career advancement for developers of all levels?

Solution

Peanut solves this by offering a comprehensive platform with competitive matchmaking, real-time coding challenges, and a dynamic community forum. Developers engage with peers, share knowledge, and learn from each other's experiences while competing to earn credits and showcase skills to potential employers. With Peanut, users can: - Compete in real-time coding challenges on one-on-one online matches - Find jobs and internships - Earn redeemable credits and badges - Connect with peers and potential employers - Share knowledge in the community forum - Showcase skills and build a reputation - Access career growth resources Peanut is the ultimate destination for developers to learn, grow, and succeed.
